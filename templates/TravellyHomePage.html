Who Are we?


Founded in February of 2021, Travelly is a free
service that aims to get you to your perfect
destination where you can avoid places abroad
that may be bad for travel. Travelly connects
travellers to great travel experiences at
great value price points.

It can be somewhat tiresome monitoring weather
conditions and flights to ensure your time abroad
is not just productive but a smooth process.

Value is a key quality we take seriously because
we want you to find the best value for flight
prices for your destination - we just concentrate
on what matters:

Great flight prices + Great weather = Value
